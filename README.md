# 👨‍💻 About Me

I am **Mert Dil**, a Physics M.Sc. student at **Technical University of Munich (TUM)** working at the intersection of **physics, data science, and machine learning**.

My work focuses on applying computational and data-driven methods to **complex physical systems**, especially in **energy and plasma research**.

I currently contribute to research projects in **fusion energy and battery systems**, combining physics-based modelling with machine learning approaches.

### Current Focus

- ⚡ **Machine learning for plasma heating and diagnostics**  
- 🔋 **Hybrid ML–physics models for battery degradation**
- 🧠 **Deep learning for scientific data analysis**
- 📊 **Scientific computing and simulation pipelines**

---

# 🔬 Research & Professional Background

- 🎓 **M.Sc. Physics – Technical University of Munich (TUM)**  
  Focus: computational physics, machine learning, and energy systems.

- 🧪 **Max Planck Institute for Plasma Physics (IPP)**  
  Working on machine-learning methods for **ion cyclotron resonance heating (ICRH)** diagnostics.
